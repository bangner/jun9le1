About

Jun9le1

I've been an artist for as long as I can remember, it has been my only consistent friend. I fled my home of Sri Lanka during the Civil War, immigrated to Canada and pursued a degree in visual arts. I received my Bachelor of Fine Arts from York University and was offered entrance into the Master's program but, feeling the pressures of "having a career", I declined. After receiving my Bachelor of Education from York University, I became a primary school teacher. I took a job in the Middle East and was able to travel all over the world, but I was missing an essential part of my life-art. So, after a few years, I knew I needed to get back in my element-back to my home.

Laced Set 1

Just Smoke It

Just Roll It

Laced is a series by Jungle 1, reflecting on two seemingly unrelated cultural influences: sneakers and drugs. Sneakers, like drugs, have become an addiction for many people in today’s society. This appetite for sneakers­­the lengths people are willing to go to get that pair, the amount of money people are willing to pay, and the sneaker related crimes people are willing to commit­­is rivaled by nothing less than our society's appetite for drugs. Being immersed in this world of nostalgic consumerism and sneaker culture ourselves, this body of work is a reflection of this prevalent cultural addiction.

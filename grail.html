The Holy Grail

The Holy Grail Package

All prints are signed and numbered 10”x13” limited, on archival German Etching Watercolor paper. Mould-made natural line Hahnemuhle German Etching is a single side coated, natural white, 100% TCF pulp, and age resistant traditional fine art paper-making your print vibrant and long-lasting.

All shirts are printed on the highest quality Vapor patented Poly-Tech Fabrics™ that has the look and feel of organic cotton without any of its shortcomings. Made of 100% spun polyester, this garment is lightweight and shrinkage, fading and wrinkling are virtually non-existent.  It also utilizes “Pure-Tech” wicking technology, which keeps you dryer and more comfortable for long periods of use.

The Grail is Art packaging at its finest. Originally conceived with the idea to create Art as an Experience, the Grail actualizes this ideology and brings the dream to life. At first glance, the Grail simply looks like an original painting that can be hung directly on a wall, but that is only the start. When the flaps are lifted and the lid is removed, the Experience has begun. Inside The Grail, a treasure trove of hangable, wearable, and swappable Art awaits you. First, a double-sided t-shirt, printed seam to seam with vivid Laced imagery and vibrant art. Then, an amazing set of die-cut stickers lay on two (2) 10”x12” inch fine art prints along with a Certificate of Authenticity. The lid can be flipped over and used as a frame to hold the print. You get to choose whether to display the original painting or to frame one of the provided prints, just by simply flipping the lid and folding the sides down to allow the magnets to engage and secure for hanging. Three rivets are conveniently cut into the back of the Grail allowing it to hang as a portrait or landscape. For the Grail's breakout debut and first exhibition, we have selected imagery from Jungle’s “Laced” series.
